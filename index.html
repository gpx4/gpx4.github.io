About GPX4

GPX4 gene has been recently identified to help cells cope with something called a oxidative stress. It has been identified through experiements that cells cannot survive without GPX4. We don't yet fully understand the impact of a partial loss of GPX4 function.


Newly Diagnosed?

If you or a loved one were recently diagnosed with a genetic mutation in GPX4, you are not alone. We are a supportive community of families working towards a treatment.
